ALFREDO

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 1/2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • salt (to taste)
  • pepper (to taste)
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp garlic (minced)
  • 8 oz fettuccine
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese
  • fresh chopped parsley (garnish)

INSTRUCTIONS

To make this chicken fettuccine alfredo recipe, youâ€™ll start by heating olive oil in a skillet and add the slices of chicken.

Season with salt and pepper and cook them 4 to 5 minutes per side.

Take the chicken from the pan and then add in the milk, broth, and garlic.

Season this with salt and pepper too. When it simmers, add your fettuccine and cook until al dente.

Then add the heavy cream and parmesan, simmering until the sauce is thick.

You can then add the chicken and toss with parsley.

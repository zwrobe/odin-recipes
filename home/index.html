Recipe For Baked Chicken Wings

Baked Chicken Wings

Baked Chicken Wings

Description

Baked chicken wings are the perfect food for Game Day and perfect for family gatherings.

Drying the wings throughly and adding a light coat of baking powder will ensure that your wings are cripsy.

This can be served with your favorite dipping sauce, I personally love my wings to be dipped in blue Blue Cheese.

Store Selection

You can purchase your chicken wings at your local grocery store of your choice.

  • Giant Food Stores

Ingredients

  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 10 chicken wings

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  2. Combine the olive oil, garlic, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a large, resealable bag; seal and shake to combine.

    Add the chicken wings; reseal and shake to coat. Arrange the chicken wings on a baking sheet.

  3. Cook the wings in the preheated oven 1 hour, or until crisp and cooked through.
